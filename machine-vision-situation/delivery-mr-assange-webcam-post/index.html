DELIVERY FOR MR. ASSANGE (Webcam Post)

Brief description

A parcel with a camera is delivered to Julian Assange at the Ecuadorian embassy in London. The images captured by the camera were transferred to a website and tweeted. Documented is the parcels journey through the Royal Mail reaching Julian Assange who performs for it from his "prison".
